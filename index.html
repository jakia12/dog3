About Me

Meet DOG, the resilient chimpanzee whose incredible journey inspires the $DOG community. Found alone as a baby in the forest during a hunting trip, DOG's early years were filled with uncertainty. Sold and kept for two years, his life took a turn for the better when he arrived at Tacugama in 2009.

From the start, DOG's dominant personality shone through, making him a leader among his adolescent peers. His path to power was challenged in 2017 with the arrival of Zack, but after Zack's passing, DOG once again found himself in the race for dominance, alongside his rivals Eglo, AJ, and Bingo.

Despite his tough exterior, DOG has a playful side. Known for his silly antics and persistent nature, he often tries to get his way through force, much to the dismay of the ladies in the group. Yet, it's this very persistence and spirit that embodies the essence of the $DOG token.
